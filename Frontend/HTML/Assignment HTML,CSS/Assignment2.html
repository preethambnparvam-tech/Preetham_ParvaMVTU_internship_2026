About the College:

  • Established in the year 2008
  • BVVS itself was started in 1906 by Gurubasava Swamiji of Bilur as a Sanskrit pathashala and over the years expanded into a large group of educational institutions across multiple districts.
  • Amruta Institute of Engineering and Management Sciences was established with the vision of providing quality technical and management education that empowers young minds to meet global challenges. The institution is committed to nurturing innovation, discipline, and ethical values while fostering academic excellence and industry-relevant skills. We strongly believe that education is the foundation for social transformation, and our goal is to produce competent professionals who contribute meaningfully to society and nation-building.
  • The institute’s R&D initiative in designing and fabricating a bio-diesel plant, sponsored by the Department of Science & Technology (DST), Government of India, was recognized as one of the best-executed projects and is being replicated in other states.
